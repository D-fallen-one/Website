ABOUT

Introduction

TechEthnics is a technical forum running under the IT Department of Inderprastha Engineering College. The seeds of this giant were sown back in 2014 by Mr. Sankalp Malhotra (Founder) and Mr. Anshul Bhatia (Founder). The motto was to focus on technical matters that may be of interest to all IT students and members, young minds to profit from the technical expertise and suggestions from all the IT Department members, brainstorming. It’s a family to those techie brains where they get the appropriate guidance by the mentors.

Event

Techethnics carry out regular college level events to keep the exuberance alive and spread the essence of it all around. Guidance from the mentors and achievements of the members are a constant source of inspiration and endless endeavours.

Webinar

TechEthnics tracks the latest technologies and imparts the knowledge to its members and other students through regular Webinars. Various programming languages are taught in this webinar and students are made aware of the latest trends. Academic assistance team provides the students with any sort of theoretical and technical help required in the academic area. Having any problems in coping with the college studies Academic Assistance Team is just a ping away.
